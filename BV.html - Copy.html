Overview

Bellum Victoria is a fictional Roblox world inspired by 18th-century musket warfare. The game focuses on regimental combat, discipline, and immersive historical roleplay.

The world features detailed military units, rich lore, and dynamic conflicts between multiple sovereign nations.

Community

Bellum Victoria is a player-driven community. Regiments hold weekly trainings, public battles and fight wars.

Most communication happens on Discord and Roblox group chats. Promotions are earned through participation and resilience.
