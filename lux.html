Overview

Luxembourg also known as Luxembourgian Empire or (LUX) for short, is a Roblox group owned by All_Rhino and was founded the 14th of february 2024 by ithap and cunuckman/luxembourg pie.

The group features military trainings as well as one on one practices, rich history, and exciting wars and battles.

Regiments

The core combat system relies on 17th-18th-century weapons:

  • Jagers Jagers are one of our two largest infantry divisions. Jagers show accuracy and stealthiness, meant to be able to engage in long ranges when needed.
  • Volunteers Volunteers are one of our two largest infantry divisions. Volunteers show great aggression and speed, meant to be able to engage in melee, close quarters combat and even mid-range when needed.
  • Dragoons The Dragoons are the only cavlary corps in Luxembourg.They show their strategic thinking and fast movement to flank and incircle the enemy. They are expected to be ready to engage on the right and the left of the enemy and support infantry as it advances.
  • The Royal Guard The Royal Guards are one of our best and versitile infantry divisions. They are feirce warriors who can manage on their own and are meant to be able to engage when needed. They are considered to have great potential, and with our help and hard training will achieve lot.
  • Artillerie Corps The Artillerie Corps are known for their deadliness on the battlefield. Artillerie Corps were one of the the most blood thirsty regiments/corps in all of lux. A well trained artillerie man is able to produce a lot of trouble for the opposing side.
